# Scoring Rubric

Default weights (sum=100):
- Traction: 20
- Product Capability: 30
- Monetization: 15
- User Sentiment: 20
- Execution Maturity: 10
- Evidence Confidence: 5

## Score definitions (1-5)
### Traction
- 1: minimal visible adoption
- 3: moderate adoption and recognizable presence
- 5: clear market traction with strong reach

### Product Capability
- 1: partial feature fit
- 3: solid fit on most core needs
- 5: excellent fit with differentiated execution

### Monetization
- 1: unclear or weak monetization
- 3: viable pricing/packaging
- 5: strong and scalable monetization model

### User Sentiment
- 1: consistently poor feedback
- 3: mixed but acceptable sentiment
- 5: consistently strong reviews and reputation

### Execution Maturity
- 1: early/unstable execution signals
- 3: stable product with regular updates
- 5: mature execution quality and operational consistency

### Evidence Confidence
- 1: sparse/low-quality sources
- 3: enough credible sources for core claims
- 5: strong, recent, multi-source evidence base
