# Source Quality Standard

## Source types
- Official: product website/docs/pricing/changelog
- Store/Directory: app stores, software directories
- Review: independent review sites and analyst writeups
- Media: reputable news or industry publications
- Research: market reports or datasets

## Confidence rules
- High: multiple recent independent sources + official corroboration
- Medium: at least one strong source + one supporting source
- Low: single weak source or stale/unverifiable evidence

## Minimum evidence per competitor
- >=3 total sources
- >=1 official source
- >=1 third-party source

## Date handling
- Record `Published Date` when available
- Always record `Access Date`
- Use absolute dates (e.g., 2026-02-25)
